About Us



We are a group of skilled specialists that take great satisfaction in restoring classic cars and have a love for collecting them. Our goal is to use genuine components and methods to restore these gorgeous machines to their original state. We recognise that vintage automotive restoration is a labour of love that calls for persistence, commitment, and attention to detail in addition to just patching up an old car. Regardless of how big or little the repair project is, our team of professionals has the skills and experience to manage it. Full restorations, partial restorations, customizations, repairs, and maintenance are just a few of the many services we provide. Whether you wish to customise your old automobile or restore it to its original state, we can help.
